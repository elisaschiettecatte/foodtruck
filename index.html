What's the concept

Once Upon a Time is an healthy footruck which proposes a new menue for breakfast, lunch, snack and dinner every week accordind to the season and local production. Every day, Once Upon A Time change of places:

  • Monday: Beaumont-sur-Oise
  • Tuesday: Presles
  • Wednesday: Maffliers
  • Thursday: Nointel
  • Friday: Viarmes
  • Saturday: L'Isle Adam
  • Sunday: * Close * we are preparing new recipes...

About Once Upon a Time

Hello, my name is Elisa Schiettecatte, I am 19 and I am living in a small town in Val d'oise: Nointel. My grand-parents taught me gardening and eat local and balanced since my childhood. I grew up with the idea that health resides in our foods and that is very important to respect our body. That why I embark on this adventure by launching a Foodtruck in my department to promote healthy, local and seasonal recipes for all the day.
